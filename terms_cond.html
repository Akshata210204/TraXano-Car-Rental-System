Traxano Car Rental - Terms & Conditions

1. Eligibility & User Requirements

The renter must be at least 23 years old.

A valid driver’s license is required.

The renter must provide a government-issued ID and a valid payment method.

If the renter fails to provide valid credentials (e.g., address proof), the booking will be automatically canceled without a refund.

2. Booking & Payments

All bookings must be made via Traxano’s online platform.

Payments must be completed before pickup.

Late returns may result in additional hourly/daily charges.

Renting is available for the next 2 months only.

3. Vehicle Pickup & Return

The vehicle must be picked up from the designated Traxano rental hub or as per the user’s selected location.

If the pick-up or drop-off location is other than the company's rental hub, a Traxano agent will deliver or collect the vehicle at the customer’s specified location.

Customers must inspect the vehicle before accepting it and report any damages.

The car must be returned with the same fuel level as when rented.

4. Rental Duration & Extensions

If the renter wishes to extend the rental period, they must notify Traxano at least 12 hours in advance.

Failure to return the car on time may result in late fees.

5. Insurance & Liability

The renter is fully responsible for the car during the rental period.

Traxano offers optional insurance coverage. This includes third-party liability and coverage for certain damages, as specified in the insurance policy details.

Basic Car Insurance: For an additional ₹100, renters can opt for Basic Insurance that covers minor damages like scratches and small dents.

Personal Accident Insurance (PAI): For an additional ₹100, renters can opt for Personal Accident Insurance, which covers medical expenses for the driver and passengers in case of an accident during the rental period.

Insurance does not cover intentional damage, reckless driving, or unauthorized drivers.

Any damages beyond normal wear & tear, not covered by the chosen insurance plan, will be charged to the renter.

6. Prohibited Uses

The rented vehicle must NOT be used for:

  • Racing, speed testing, or illegal activities.
  • Towing, carrying hazardous materials, or off-road driving.
  • Allowing unauthorized persons to drive the car.

7. Cancellation & Refunds

Full refunds are issued if canceled at least 24 hours before the booking start time.

Partial refunds may be given for cancellations within 24 hours.

No refund for last-minute cancellations or no-shows.

8. Fuel & Toll Charges

Renters are responsible for fuel costs.

Any toll charges, parking tickets, or traffic violations incurred during the rental period must be paid by the renter.

9. Emergency Assistance & Breakdown

If the vehicle breaks down, contact Traxano Support immediately.

Roadside assistance is available for flat tires, jump-starts, and minor repairs.

If a replacement vehicle is needed, one will be provided based on availability.

10. Booking Updates

Updating a booking is allowed only once.

A non-refundable updation fee of ₹100 will apply.

If the updated rental price is lower than the original price, a 10% deduction will be applied on the difference, and the remaining amount will be refunded to the renter.

11. Privacy Policy

Traxano collects user data for identity verification and rental tracking.

We do not share personal data with third parties without user consent.

12. Violation of Terms & Penalties

Any violation of these terms may result in:

  1. Additional fines or termination of rental.
  2. Permanent suspension from using Traxano.
  3. Legal action if necessary.
